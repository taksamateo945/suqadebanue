Beyonce Releases New Single &quot;Runnin' (Lose It All)&quot;: See the Music Vid

Publish date: 2024-04-05

She already runs the world, and now Beyonce is simply “Runnin’.” The pop diva, 34, has spent the past year mostly out of the spotlight following the smash success of her self-titled visual album, which had a surprise drop in December 2013. 

But now, in collaboration with British DJ Naughty Boy (of Zayn Malik feud fame), Queen Bey has released a new single, “Runnin’ (Lose It All),” which also features Arrow Benjamin.
