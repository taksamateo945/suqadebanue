Christina Aguilera, Sofia Carson, Bella Thorne at the 22nd annual Latin Grammy Awards in Las Vegas

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Christina Aguilera, Sofia Carson, and Bella Thorne were just some of the stars attending the 22nd annual Latin GRAMMY Awards in Las Vegas on Thursday evening.

Aguilera looked like a total vixen as she rocked the red carpet at the MGM Grand Garden Arena ahead of the ceremony.

The 40-year-old superstar slipped into a black curve-clinging gown with vinyl puffed sleeves and sexy arm-length gloves.

The dress also featured a lengthy train and a cat-ear bust that allowed the Stronger singer to flaunt her ample assets.

Christina traded her signature blonde strands for a long, auburn wig that she styled in a mod half up half down style.

A silver chain with an array of cross pendants fell into her cleavage and a pair of matching dangly earrings hung from her ears as she posed. 

As for makeup, the Grammy Award-winner rocked an over-lined lip and a dramatic smokey eye with some champagne shimmer across the lids.

They’re keeping the same coronavirus protocols in place that were used for last year’s ceremony, which means ‘crew, staff, performers and presenters must wear a mask at all times during rehearsals, and team members must take a PCR nasal test the day before entering the venue,’ according to the NY Times.

‘The safety of our attendees, staff and artists is of the utmost and top concern of the Latin Recording Academy and Univision Communications Inc.,’ stressed chief executive of the Latin Recording Academy Manuel Abud in a statement ahead of Thursday’s show. 

‘While some of the regulations have eased, the Latin Recording Academy and Univision will continue to exercise strict precautions to ensure the safety and well-being of all attendees.’
