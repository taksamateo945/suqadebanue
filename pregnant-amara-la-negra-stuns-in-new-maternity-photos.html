PREGNANT AMARA LA NEGRA STUNS IN NEW MATERNITY PHOTOS

Publish date: 2024-05-11

Pregnant Amara La Negra is glowing and fabulous! The LHHMIA star recently shared pictures and a video clip from her latest photoshoot.

Amara channeled her modeling energy for the photo session. “The best stage of my life,” the celebrity captioned underneath one candid that featured her striking a pose while wrapped in a white sheet. “They chose me,” Amara said of her unborn twins. “I am the chosen one,” the LHHMIA star told her Instagram fans.

Amara La Negra announced her expectancy last November. The Reality TV star took to social media with a positive pregnancy test to share the exciting news of her family’s expansion.

“I am so excited to finally share that we have twins on the way,” Amara told her Instagram followers. “Thank you @clearblue for being a part of our journey and giving me the most amazing news,” the LHHMIA star wrote online.

“There is nothing better than seeing the word PREGNANT,” Amara exclaimed. “Even though I’m scared and nervous, especially after having a miscarriage, I am also excited and have a roller coaster of emotions but it truly is a Blessing.”

Amara further shared her excitement about pregnancy during an exclusive interview with PEOPLE: En Espanol. “In my case, I woke up pregnant like the Virgin Mary. I woke up pregnant and that’s all I remember,” the celebrity said. “I’m going to be a single mother,” Amara continued.

“I know my babies are going to depend on me,” the LHHMIA star added. “Father is not the one who begets, he is the one who breeds. At the time, if God gives me the blessing of finding the right man, who supports me, who accepts me with my children, well, well received. But I feel blessed and I am so happy that I even forget that [about being a single mother]. I am more focused on my babies.”

Amara La Negra is expecting her twins to arrive in the coming months.
