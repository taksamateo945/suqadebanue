Ashley Ortega’s Boyfriend: The Filipino-German Actress Is Reportedly Single Right Now!

Ashley Ortega (@ashleyortega) currently does not have a boyfriend. However, she recently admitted that Juancho Trivio, who is happily married to Joyce Pring, was her first love and first boyfriend. Ortega made the revelation on the March 17 episode of Fast Talk with Boy Abunda, when TV host Boy Abunda asked if she had ever fallen in love with a leading man before. Ortega and Trivio have previously appeared together in several GMA projects, most notably in episodes of the anthology series Maynila and InstaDad.

During the interview, the Hearts on Ice star also discussed her recent split from Lucena City mayor Mark Alcala, which she described as a mutual decision. Ortega announced their split earlier this month, noting that they'd been separated since last year. She stated,

I fought for our relationship until the very end. We had a mutual agreement and I respected his decision. We don’t want to force ourselves if we know it won’t work because if we know it won’t work, why should we even fight for it?

The actress also admitted that their relationship had communication issues, but she clarified that she has no animosity toward the politician. Later, adding that their breakup had been six months, she explained,

Our distance from each other became a problem. We lost our connection and we had problems with communication. I don’t know if we’re friends but I have nothing against him.
