Who is Ethan Dolan:

Ethan Dolan is a famous YouTube Star. He was born on December 16, 1999 and his birthplace is New Jersey. His Vine channel accumulated more than 3.2 million followers and the twins’ collaborative YouTube channel has amassed over 10 million subscribers. In 2018, he became known as part of the Sister Squad alongside his brother and others.

Ethan is originated from United States. He and Grayson have made videos with numerous other social stars such as Kenny Holland, Amanda Cerny, Jack Dail, Jake Paul and many others.
