Lisa Presley Biography

Lisa Marie Presley was born on February 1, 1968, at Baptist Memorial Hospital-Memphis in Memphis, Tennessee, to Elvis Presley and Priscilla Presley. Her parents divorced later, and she moved in with her mother. Lisa Marie became a joint heir to her father’s estate after her father died in 1977, when she was only nine years old, along with her grandfather Vernon Elvis Presley and her great grandmother Minnie Mae Hood Presley.

Following the deaths of her grandfather in 1979 and great-grandmother in 1980, she became the sole heir and inherited Graceland. Lisa inherited the estate, which had grown to $100 million, on her 25th birthday in 1993; however, in 2004, she sold 85 percent of her father’s estate. Lisa saw her first rock concert in the late 1970s when she saw Queen at The Forum in Los Angeles; after the show, she gave Freddie Mercury her father’s scarf and expressed her love for theatrics.
